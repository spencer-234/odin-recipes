McDonald's Fries Home

Recreate the famous McDonald's fries at home!

This recipe, by Kenneth Smith, teaches you how to recreate the famous McDonald's fries from the comfort of your own kitchen.
It will take a total of 1 hr 15 mins to complete this dish and it will produce 4 servings. Let's get started!

Ingredients:

  • 8 potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/4 inch fries
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons corn syrup
  • 1 quart canola oil, or oil of choice
  • sea salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Place potatoes in a bowl and cover with water; let sit for 5 minutes. Drain and pat dry.
  2. Place potatoes in a bowl and cover with boiling water; add sugar and corn syrup and mix well.
    Place bowl in refrigerator and let sit for 5 minutes. Drain and pat dry.
  3. Spread potatoes out in a baking dish, cover with plastic wrap, and freeze for 30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  5. Working in batches, fry potatoes in hot oil for 2 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate
    to dry and let cool for 15 minutes.
  6. Working in batches again, fry potatoes in hot oil until browned and crispy for 5 to 7 minues and season.
